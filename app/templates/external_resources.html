Free Therapy / Emotional Support Lines
  • SAMHSA's National Helpline
    1-800-662-HELP (4357)
    Free, confidential help for mental health and substance use issues.
    Available 24/7, 365 days a year.
    They can connect you to free or low-cost counseling services locally too.
  • Crisis Text Line
    Text HELLO to 741741
    Chat with a trained Crisis Counselor.
    Free and 24/7 anywhere in the U.S.
  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) Helpline
    1-800-950-NAMI (6264)
    Mon-Fri, 10 a.m. – 10 p.m. Eastern Time.
    They give mental health guidance, emotional support, and help you find therapy.
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
    Just call 988 (like dialing 911, but for mental health).
    For any mental health crisis — not just suicide.
    Available 24/7.
    You can talk about anxiety, depression, panic attacks, overwhelming stress, anything.
